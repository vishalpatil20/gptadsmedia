How It Works

The anatomy of ChatGPT conversational ads

OpenAI utilizes a structured, privacy-preserving sponsored template served dynamically based on user intent.

01
Native Format
Sponsored links appear as clean, unobtrusive citation blocks directly under relevant AI answers, blending seamlessly into the natural flow of the conversation.
02
Context-Triggered
Ads are matched semantically based on what users are actively talking about—triggering recommendations when they ask for options, solutions, or category comparisons.
03
High Attention
Since users actively read LLM responses line-by-line, conversational ad placements achieve near-zero ad blindness and capture high-intent buyer focus.
